What is PubGame?

Inspired by one of the most famous party game of USA, PUB GAME was born as a metaverse of comic and exclusive NFTs games, which stand out for their exceptionality in stimulating the fun side of life.

Beer pong is the first gaming modality that will be introduced in the new crypto-universe;

With the main objective of hitting the balls inside your opponent's drinking cups. With each hit you make, your opponent must drink the drink from the cup. The game is over when one of the contestants hits all of his opponent's cups or his opponent's drunk bar is full.

Art X Collectibles

NFT holders will earn tradeable NFTs and fungible tokens simply from playing the game. You can also challenge your friends!
